Requesting a Tutor
1. Go into your Bootcampspot Dashboard
2. Click the "Chat" icon (bottom right)
3. Click Support Ticket
4. A drop down menu will appear. Fill out the request form. Make sure you choose "Request a Tutor" as the category!
5. An email will come within a day or so with details about to schedule tutoring sessions
